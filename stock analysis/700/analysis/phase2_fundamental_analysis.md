## 1. Business Model Analysis

### Core Business Overview

Tencent operates as an integrated digital ecosystem with four main revenue streams:

| Business Segment | Revenue Share | Description | Moat Type |
|------------------|---------------|-------------|-----------|
| **VAS (Gaming + Social)** | 50% | Games, virtual items, subscriptions | Network Effects + IP |
| **FinTech & Enterprise** | 30% | Payments, cloud, business services | Ecosystem Lock-in |
| **Marketing Services** | 19% | Advertising across platforms | Data + Traffic |
| **Others** | 1% | Content, licensing, etc. | - |

## 3. Competitive Moat Analysis

### Moat Framework

| Moat Type | Strength | Evidence |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| **Network Effects** | VERY STRONG | 1.4B WeChat users, social graphs |
| **Switching Costs** | VERY STRONG | Years of social connections |
| **Intangible Assets (IP)** | STRONG | Gaming franchises, brands |
| **Cost Advantages** | MODERATE | Scale, but not primary moat |
| **Efficient Scale** | MODERATE | Market dominance in gaming |

### Competitive Position by Segment

#### Gaming (50% of VAS Revenue)
| Factor | Tencent's Position |
|--------|-------------------|
| Market Share | #1 in China, #1 Globally |
| Key Franchises | Honor of Kings, PUBG, Valorant |
| Pipeline | Rich (Honor of Kings World, Delta Force) |
| Moat | Strong IP + Distribution |
| International | 31% of gaming revenue |

#### Social/WeChat
| Factor | Tencent's Position |
|--------|-------------------|
| Market Share | Dominant (1.4B MAU) |
| Competitive Threat | Low (social network effects) |
| Monetization | Accelerating (Video Account, Shop) |
| Moat | Impregnable |

#### Advertising
| Factor | Tencent's Position |
|--------|-------------------|
| Market Share | #2 in China (after ByteDance) |
| Growth | +21% YoY (outpacing market) |
| Advantage | Private domain data |
| Moat | Data + Ecosystem |

#### FinTech/Cloud
| Factor | Tencent's Position |
|--------|-------------------|

| Moat | Ecosystem integration |
