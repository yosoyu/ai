# Due Diligence Checklist

Comprehensive checklist for researching a stock.

---

## Company Basics

- [ ] Company name and ticker
- [ ] Exchange listed
- [ ] Industry/sector
- [ ] Market cap
- [ ] Employees
- [ ] Founded year
- [ ] HQ location
- [ ] Company website
- [ ] Investor relations page

## Financial Statements

### Income Statement
- [ ] Revenue (5-year trend)
- [ ] Gross profit and margin
- [ ] Operating profit and margin
- [ ] Net profit and margin
- [ ] EPS (basic and diluted)
- [ ] Revenue composition by segment
- [ ] Revenue by geography

### Balance Sheet
- [ ] Total assets
- [ ] Total liabilities
- [ ] Shareholders' equity
- [ ] Cash and equivalents
- [ ] Total debt
- [ ] Current assets/liabilities
- [ ] Book value per share

### Cash Flow Statement
- [ ] Operating cash flow
- [ ] Investing cash flow
- [ ] Financing cash flow
- [ ] Free cash flow
- [ ] Capex
- [ ] Dividends paid
- [ ] Share repurchases
