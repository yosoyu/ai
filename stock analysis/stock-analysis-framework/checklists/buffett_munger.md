# Buffett-Munger Quality Investing Checklist

Use this checklist throughout the analysis process.

---

## Business Quality

### Simplicity
- [ ] Can I explain how this company makes money in 1 minute?
- [ ] Is the business model simple and understandable?
- [ ] Can I predict what this company will look like in 10 years?

### Competitive Advantage (Moat)
- [ ] Does it have a durable competitive advantage?
- [ ] Can competitors easily replicate the business?
- [ ] Does it have pricing power?
- [ ] Are there high switching costs for customers?
- [ ] Is there a network effect?

### Financial Evidence of Moat
- [ ] Is ROE consistently >15% over 5+ years?
- [ ] Are margins stable or expanding?
- [ ] Is there consistent free cash flow?
- [ ] Does it earn above its cost of capital?

---

## Management Quality

### Integrity
- [ ] Is management honest in communications?
- [ ] Do they admit mistakes?
- [ ] Are there any governance red flags?
- [ ] Have they treated minority shareholders fairly?
- [ ] Any failed privatization attempts?

### Alignment
- [ ] Does management own significant stock?
- [ ] Are their interests aligned with shareholders?
- [ ] Is compensation reasonable and performance-based?

### Capital Allocation
- [ ] Do they have a track record of good capital allocation?
- [ ] Do they buy back shares when undervalued?
- [ ] Do they make sensible acquisitions?
- [ ] Is dividend policy appropriate?

### Track Record
- [ ] How long has management been in place?
- [ ] What is their historical performance?
- [ ] Have they navigated crises well?

---

## Financial Health

### Balance Sheet
- [ ] Is debt manageable (D/E <50% for most industries)?
- [ ] Is there adequate liquidity (current ratio >1.5)?
- [ ] Is there a net cash position or manageable debt?

### Profitability
- [ ] Is ROE consistently >15%?
- [ ] Is ROIC > cost of capital?
- [ ] Are margins above industry average?
- [ ] Is earnings quality high (cash vs accrual)?

### Cash Flow
- [ ] Is free cash flow positive?
- [ ] Does operating cash flow cover net income?
- [ ] Is capex maintenance vs growth appropriate?

### Consistency
- [ ] Are earnings consistent and predictable?
- [ ] Is there a history of meeting guidance?
- [ ] Are there no big surprises?

---

## Valuation

### Margin of Safety
- [ ] Is there a margin of safety >25%?
- [ ] Am I buying at a discount to intrinsic value?
- [ ] Is the price attractive relative to quality?

### Intrinsic Value
- [ ] Have I calculated intrinsic value?
- [ ] What valuation method(s) did I use?
- [ ] Are my assumptions conservative?

### Worst Case
- [ ] What is the worst-case scenario?
- [ ] Can the company survive a downturn?
- [ ] What would cause permanent loss of capital?

---

## Psychological Checklist

### Circle of Competence
- [ ] Do I understand this business?
- [ ] Is it within my circle of competence?
- [ ] Am I being honest about what I don't know?

### Behavioral Biases
- [ ] Am I being influenced by recent price movement?
- [ ] Am I falling in love with the stock?
- [ ] Am I confirming my existing beliefs?
- [ ] Have I considered the bear case seriously?

### Invert, Always Invert
- [ ] What could kill this investment?
- [ ] What am I missing?
- [ ] Why might the market be right about the low price?

---

## Final Questions

Before investing, answer:
1. **Why am I buying?** (Clear thesis)
2. **What would make me sell?** (Exit criteria)
3. **What is my time horizon?** (Patience required)
4. **What is my position size?** (Risk management)
