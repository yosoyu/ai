
- 3-sided network effects (consumers, merchants, riders)

**Currently Threatened:**
- Competitors buying market share
- Low switching costs for consumers
- Deep-pocketed rivals (Alibaba, JD)

## 2.6 Investment Thesis Summary

### The Bull Case
1. Dominant market position (still 50%+ share)
2. Strong cash position (HK$141B war chest)
3. Network effects still intact
4. Low P/S (1.38x) for a platform business
5. Founder-controlled, long-term oriented
6. International expansion (KeeTa) showing success

1. **Massive profit warning** - 2025 swing to huge loss
2. **Intense competition** - Market share eroding
3. **Price war** - Burning cash to defend position
4. **Regulatory risk** - Government intervention possible
5. **No dividend** - Returns depend on stock appreciation
6. **Moat under attack** - Deep-pocketed competitors
