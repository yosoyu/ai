## 4.5 Investment Thesis Statement

### Short Form
> **"Buy a portfolio of quality Chinese assets at 45 cents on the dollar, while collecting 4-8% yield, betting that governance improvements will narrow the discount."**

### Detailed Thesis

**Why Buy:**
1. The portfolio contains real, valuable assets (CMB alone worth ~current market cap)
2. 55% discount provides massive margin of safety
3. 2024 governance victory shows shareholders can effect change
4. Dividend provides income while waiting
5. Multiple potential catalysts (IPOs, fee reduction, privatization)

**Why Wait/Cautious:**
1. Discount has persisted for years
2. Management has historically extracted high fees
3. Unlisted assets valuation uncertain
4. No guarantee discount will narrow
5. Requires patience and monitoring

**Catalyst Path:**
1. Permanent fee reduction confirmed
2. IPO exits of unlisted investments
3. Potential privatization at NAV premium
4. General China market recovery
